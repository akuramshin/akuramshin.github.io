description: "From September 2024 to August 2026, I completed my MSc under the supervision of [Glen Berseth](https://neo-x.github.io/), where I worked on improving language-following capabilities in VLA models. My research included developing [TREAD](https://akuramshin.github.io/tread), a VLM-based data augmentation approach for improving robustness to language instructions, contributing to [RoboArena](https://robo-arena.github.io/) for benchmarking and evaluating manipulation policies in real-world robotic settings, and exploring scalable inference-time reasoning for VLAs through the [VLAPS](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2508.12211?) project, which combines VLA policies with search-based planning methods.

In parallel, I developed a full teleoperation and data-collection stack for a Franka robot arm integrated with a LEAP dexterous hand, enabling collection of demonstrations for training and evaluation of manipulation policies."
